Listening to music is great for your health

Listening to music is a hobby because it can be very relaxing and enjoyable. According to Scientific American, listening to music helps reduce stress and anxiety, improves mental functions like memory and reasoning, promotes cardiovascular health, increases pain tolerance, reduces depression which improves mood. It also boosts the immune system, improves sleep and has been proven to have a positive effect on the body. In addition to all of these benefits, listening to music can give a person a sense of relaxation and self-control.
